# 14R-3 — Operating drivers and financial bridges

## Actions

For each case:

1. qualify the required drivers;
2. compute segment revenue and gross profit;
3. eliminate cross-cutting overlap;
4. reconcile to consolidated statements;
5. build working-capital, cash-tax, capex, and free-cash-flow bridges;
6. disclose proxy and residual shares;
7. run sensitivities;
8. reroute missing drivers to T1/T2 rather than filling them with generic growth rates.

## Exit criteria

- revenue coverage ≥80%;
- gross-profit coverage ≥80%;
- required driver coverage passes contract policy;
- overlap, statement, working-capital, and cash-flow bridges pass;
- deterministic rerun passes.
