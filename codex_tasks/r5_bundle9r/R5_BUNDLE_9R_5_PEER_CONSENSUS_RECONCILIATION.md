# 9R.5 — Peer and consensus reconciliation

## Goal

Use operating comparability—not merely industry labels—to determine peer eligibility and compare the model with external estimates.

## Required work

- Reconcile each peer's annual operating metrics to official issuer disclosures where possible.
- Record business overlap, cooling/liquid-cooling purity, margin structure, growth, ROE and market-date consistency.
- Keep `ranking_allowed: false` while the peer set is low confidence.
- Record THS consensus distribution by year and institution count; keep single-broker PDFs separately.
- Normalize share basis and forecast periods before EPS comparison.

## Outputs

`R5_bundle9r_peer_operating_reconciliation.yaml` and `R5_bundle9r_consensus_comparison.csv`.
