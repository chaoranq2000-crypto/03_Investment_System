# 9R.6 — Reverse and scenario valuation

## Goal

Answer what operating performance the current market value requires, and what value ranges follow from the three scenarios.

## Required methods

- **Reverse valuation**: infer required 2027/2028 attributable profit, margin and/or segment economics under transparent multiple assumptions.
- **Scenario valuation**: bear/base/bull ranges with explicit earnings anchor, multiple range, date, shares and market-cap reconciliation.

## Method eligibility

- Do not enable SOTP unless standalone segment economics and overlap eliminations are reviewable.
- Do not enable DCF unless FCFF, net debt, discount rate and terminal assumptions are all reviewed and traced.
- Low-confidence peer multiples may be context but cannot be the sole valuation anchor.
- No target price or trading instruction.

## Outputs

`R5_bundle9r_reverse_valuation.yaml`, `R5_bundle9r_scenario_valuation.yaml`, and the valuation block of `R5_bundle9r_model_pack.yaml`.
