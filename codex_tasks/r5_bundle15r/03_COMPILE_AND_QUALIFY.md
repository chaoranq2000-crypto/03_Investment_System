# 15R-3 — Compile qualifications and resolve conflicts

## Actions

1. Run Bundle 15R with physical source verification enabled.
2. Inspect every case audit, the evidence request queue, and the conflict ledger.
3. Resolve duplicate reviewed records only when their normalized values and
   operating definitions agree.
4. Fail closed on conflicting reviewed values; do not choose a preferred number
   without a new review decision.
5. Complete revenue and gross-profit overlap reconciliation.
6. Complete driver-to-statement, working-capital, and cash-flow bridges.
7. Satisfy the case-specific revenue and gross-profit coverage thresholds.
8. Pass at least one independently eligible valuation method.
9. Hash-bind the semantic result and generation locks.

## Exit criteria

Each case is either `qualification_ready_for_bundle14r` or has precise owned
backflow. Aggregate score or another company's result cannot compensate.
