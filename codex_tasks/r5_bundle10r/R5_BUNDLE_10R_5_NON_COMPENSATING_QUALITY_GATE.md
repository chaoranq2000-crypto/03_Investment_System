# 10R.5 — Non-compensating Reader quality gate

## Goal

Make core research failures non-compensating.

## Core sections

- financial quality;
- segment economics;
- industry and competition;
- forecast and scenarios;
- valuation and market-implied expectations;
- risks and falsification;
- conclusion and watchlist.

## Acceptance

- Failure of any core section blocks candidate readiness regardless of total score.
- Forecast failure cannot be offset by eight other sections.
- Score starts from zero and rewards verified capabilities.
- Truthfulness blockers, candidate blockers, and warnings remain separate.
- Thin-report, stale-generation, claim-boundary, and action-language fixtures fail closed.
