# 10R.1 — Reader input and claim contract

## Goal

Create a reviewed Reader input pack that keeps fact, management comment, estimate, analyst view, analytical inference, and unknown disclosure boundaries distinct.

## Required work

- Register each material statement with display references, claim type, confidence, and falsification condition.
- Carry Bundle 9R assumptions into the Reader as estimates, never issuer guidance.
- Keep consensus data as `analyst_view` or `estimate`.
- Keep standalone liquid-cooling economics non-additive and explicitly unquantified unless issuer disclosure becomes available.
- Keep low-confidence peers unranked and explain the comparability limitation.
- Reject target-price, buy/sell/hold, position-sizing, and timing instructions.

## Acceptance

- Every core section has traceable facts and explicit uncertainty.
- No claim boundary is strengthened during narrative generation.
- Reader input declares the exact model generation it consumes.
