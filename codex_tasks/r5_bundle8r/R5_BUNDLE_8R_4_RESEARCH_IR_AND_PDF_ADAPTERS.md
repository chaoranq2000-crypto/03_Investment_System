# R5 Bundle 8R.4 — Research, IR and PDF adapters

## Scope
Complete individual and industry report metadata, PDF download/archive, THS consensus EPS and CNINFO IRM.

## Boundaries
- Single-broker values are analyst views, not consensus.
- Consensus requires methodology and contributor count when available.
- IRM answers are management comments and never issuer-reported segment revenue by themselves.
- PDF provenance must link metadata → downloaded bytes → text/page map.

## Acceptance
The 002837 pilot produces an industry research inventory, archived PDFs where publicly accessible, an estimate-distribution pack and an IR question/answer pack with explicit boundaries.
